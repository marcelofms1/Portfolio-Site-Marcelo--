About me

My name is Marcelo Santos. I was born in Brazil and have lived in the US most of my life. I am an artist and a programmer, I love anything to do with creativity and imagination.

My Process:

I like to start by speaking to the client to get an insight into what it is they are looking for. I take a look at the information and content that will be going onto the page such as text, images etc. I study or build a wireframe that will suit the style of the client and the style of their content. Staying in communication is important to me, it allows me to get feedback from the client so I can make changes accordingly.
